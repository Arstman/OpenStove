steps:
        - title: "Make the Khmeli Suneli Spice Mix (Optional)"
          actions:
            - "In a mortar and pestle or spice grinder, combine dried coriander seeds, blue fenugreek seeds, and marigold petals. Grind to a powder. Alternatively, use pre-made khmeli suneli spice mix."

        - title: "Brown the Beef"
          actions:
           - "In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Season beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces, with salt and pepper. Add the beef to the hot oil and brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes."

        - title: "Sauté the Onions and Garlic"
          actions:
           - "Transfer the browned beef to a plate. Add butter to the pot and melt it over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant."

        - title: "Deglaze the Pan and Add Tomatoes"
          actions:
           - "Pour in beef broth or water, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Add  diced tomatoes, undrained, and bring to a simmer."

        - title: "Simmer with Spices and Herbs"
          actions:
           - "Return the browned beef to the pot. Add the prepared khmeli suneli spice mix (or store-bought), bay leaf, and a pinch of dried red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference). Simmer for 30 minutes, or until the beef is tender."

        - title: "Cook the Rice"
          actions:
           - "In a separate pot, bring water or beef broth to a boil. Add rinsed white rice and a pinch of salt. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and fluffy."

        - title: "Thicken the Soup (Optional)"
          actions:
            - "In a small bowl, whisk together tomato paste with a little bit of water to create a slurry. Gradually stir the slurry into the soup until it reaches your desired consistency. You can also add a teaspoon of cornstarch mixed with water for thickening."

        - title: "Assemble and Serve"
          actions:
           - "Stir the cooked rice into the soup pot. Remove the bay leaf. Taste and adjust seasonings with salt, pepper, and additional red pepper flakes if desired."
           - "Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro and parsley. Serve hot alongside crusty bread for a satisfying meal."
